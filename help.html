Edutree's Support Center




This is the official page where you can share all your queries, feedback, complaints or any concerns you may have about EDUTREE'S learning programs.

As a self-regulatory body, EDUTREE promises a resolution of your grievance within 15 days by our dedicated team.

Any refund-related queries, product feedback or any other concerns you have can be shared through this portal. You will be assigned a ticket regarding the concern that you raise. You can quote the same ticket number in case you call our helpline number for the same query. At EDUTREE, we strive to address and resolve issues in the shortest possible time and in the most seamless way. We aim to deliver the best learning experience and level of service to each and every student.

In case of any grievance, please fill the form below and share your concern with us.
Alternatively, you can also write to us at:
